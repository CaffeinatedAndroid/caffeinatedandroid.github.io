What is the Digital Garden?

This is a place to log and share my engineering and computer science projects.

Currently the main project taking up my time is a hardware overhaul of a Robi robot from japan, designed by robotics expert Tomotaka Takahashi. It uses robotic bus servos and has a friendly aesthetic originally inspired by the works of Osamu Tezuka. which makes it a perfect base to develop a testbed platform for machine learning and social robotics. However It needs a major hardware overhaul to support AI research is which is where my project comes in. It also serves as an alternative path to learn more advanced mechatronics and a platform to apply what I learn in my studies.
